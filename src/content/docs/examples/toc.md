This page demonstrates the table of contents (TOC) feature. Scroll through the page to see the TOC highlight the current section.

## How the TOC Works

The table of contents is automatically generated from headings on the page. It includes:

- `h2` headings (like "How the TOC Works")
- `h3` headings (like subsections)
- `h4` headings (for more detail)

The TOC appears on the right side of the page on desktop and is accessible via the TOC button on mobile.
